Purpose of this page

This OSF project page describes our procedures as part of the Perspectives on Psychological Science Registered Replication Report of the following study:

  • Finkel, E. J., Rusbult, C. E., Kumashiro, M., & Hannon, P. A. (2002). Dealing with betrayal in close relationships: Does commitment promote forgiveness? Journal of Personality and Social Psychology, 82, 956-974.

Please consult the official protocol and RRR project page for details of the experiment's methodology. Details of our implementation of this protocol are described in the Implementation component.
